Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, share, and protect your information.

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Hawcus CRM ("Hawcus", "we", "us", or "our"), operated by Social Eagle, provides a customer relationship management (CRM) platform that helps businesses capture, manage, and communicate with their leads and customers. This Privacy Policy explains what data we process and how we protect it when you use our web application at app.hawcus.com and related services (the "Service").

Quick summary: We process business and lead data on behalf of our customers (businesses that use Hawcus). We do not sell your data. You can request access to or deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@hawcus.com.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Name, email address, phone number, company name, and role of users who sign in to Hawcus.
  • Authentication data (securely hashed passwords, session and refresh tokens).

Lead and contact data

  • Contact details of leads and customers (name, email, phone, address, and enquiry details) that our customers collect through forms, imports, or integrations.
  • Conversation history, notes, tags, appointments, and activity records associated with a lead.

Data from Meta platforms (Facebook, Instagram, WhatsApp)

  • Facebook Lead Ads: when a business connects its Facebook Page, we retrieve lead form submissions (the fields the person entered) so they appear in that business's CRM.
  • WhatsApp Business Platform: when a business connects its WhatsApp Business Account, we send and receive messages on its behalf and store the message content and phone numbers involved to display conversations in the inbox.
  • Pages: basic Page information and the list of Pages a user manages, used only to let the user choose which Page to connect.

Usage and technical data

  • Log data such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps, used for security and to operate the Service.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the CRM and its features.
  • To deliver lead data and messages from connected integrations into the correct business account.
  • To send transactional notifications (for example, login codes, staff invitations, follow-up reminders, and workflow emails).
  • To secure the Service, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

We process lead and customer data as a data processor on behalf of the business (our customer) that controls that data. We do not use it for our own marketing.

3. How We Share Information

  • With your business account: data is visible to authorized users within the same business, according to their role and permissions.
  • Service providers: we use trusted infrastructure and email providers (for hosting, database, and message delivery) that process data only on our instructions.
  • Meta platforms: when you send WhatsApp messages or use Meta integrations, data is exchanged with Meta as required to deliver the feature, subject to Meta's own terms.
  • Legal: where required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as the business account is active or as needed to provide the Service. When a business disconnects an integration or closes its account, the associated data is deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period, unless we are required to keep it for legal reasons.

5. Data Security

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) for all traffic.
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive credentials such as integration access tokens.
  • Role-based access controls and tenant isolation so each business only sees its own data.

6. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access, correct, or export your data.
  • Request deletion of your data - see our Data Deletion instructions.
  • Disconnect any integration at any time from within the app.

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@hawcus.com.

7. Children's Privacy

The Service is intended for businesses and is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. International Users

The Service is operated from India. By using it, you consent to the processing of your data in accordance with this policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the latest version. Material changes will be communicated within the app or by email.
