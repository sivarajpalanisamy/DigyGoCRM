Data Deletion Instructions

How to request deletion of your data from Hawcus CRM.

Last updated: 9 July 2026

Hawcus CRM (operated by Social Eagle) respects your right to control your data. This page explains how to have your personal data - including any data obtained through Facebook, Instagram, or WhatsApp integrations - deleted from our systems.

Option 1 - Delete from within the app

If you signed in to Hawcus, you can remove data directly:

  • Disconnect an integration: go to Integrations and disconnect Facebook or WhatsApp. This stops further data collection and removes the stored access tokens for that connection.
  • Delete records: leads, contacts, and conversations can be deleted from their respective pages by users with the required permission.
  • Close your account: contact us (below) to fully delete your account and all associated data.

Option 2 - Request deletion by email

Send us a deletion request and we will process it for you:

  1. Email hello@hawcus.com from the email address associated with your account.
  2. Use the subject line Data Deletion Request.
  3. Tell us which data to delete - your whole account, a specific business, or data received from a specific Facebook Page or WhatsApp number.
  4. We will verify your identity, delete the data, and confirm by email.
Email a deletion request
Facebook / Instagram / WhatsApp data: if a business connected a Meta account to Hawcus, any lead data or messages we received through that connection are deleted when the integration is disconnected or when you submit a deletion request as described above.

What gets deleted

  • Your account profile and login credentials.
  • Leads, contacts, conversations, notes, and activity linked to your request.
  • Access tokens and data received from connected Facebook Pages and WhatsApp Business Accounts.

Timeframe

We complete verified deletion requests within 30 days. Some information may be retained where required by law (for example, financial or security records), after which it is also deleted.
